The Council

The México–India
Business Council.

MIBC is the first dedicated bilateral business council for the México–India corridor, headquartered in India.

Established with the support of the Government of México, the Council serves as the institutional platform for commercial engagement between the two nations. MIBC operates at the intersection of government policy and private enterprise. Our mandate extends beyond trade promotion to encompass investment facilitation, market entry support, and delegation coordination.

What We Do.

Our Vision, Mission & Edge.

VISION

To establish the México–India trade corridor as a strategic pillar of both nations' economic engagement, facilitating USD 50 billion in bilateral trade and investment over the next decade.

MISSION

To serve as the institutional platform for México–India commercial engagement by providing intelligence-driven facilitation, government coordination, and market entry support to enterprises of both nations.

OUR EDGE

MIBC fills a critical institutional gap as the only dedicated México–India business council in India. Led by a CEO with 15 years of bilateral trade and investment experience, we provide end-to-end support from feasibility to market launch.

Institutional Backing

  • Established with Government of México Support

    MIBC is established with the support of the Government of México, through its Consulate General in Mumbai.

  • Institutional Coordination

    The Council works in coordination with Mexican trade and investment agencies to deliver institutional-grade facilitation to enterprises of both nations.
