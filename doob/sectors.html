Sectors

Focus Sectors

MIBC delivers strategic support in sectors where bilateral trade is growing, investment is accelerating, and collaboration is poised to scale.

Category 1

Indian Investment into México

Information Technology

Opportunity: Nearshore delivery for North America via USMCA

Key Activities: Software, IT Services, BPO, Engineering R&D

Why México: US time zones, lower costs, USMCA access

Indian Players: TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL, Tech Mahindra

Pharmaceuticals

Opportunity: Generic manufacturing & LATAM access

Key Activities: APIs, generics, clinical research, vaccines

Why México: LATAM gateway, strong regulation, rising healthcare

Indian Players: Sun Pharma, Dr. Reddy’s, Lupin, Cipla

Automotive & Components

Opportunity: EV supply chain & Tier-1 operations

Key Activities: EV parts, wiring harnesses, castings

Why México: Detroit OEM access, auto cluster, USMCA rules

Indian Players: Motherson Sumi, Tata AutoComp, Bharat Forge

Category 2

Mexican Exports to India

Energy & Minerals

Products: Crude oil, refined fuels, critical minerals

Trade Value: Largest México→India export segment

Opportunity: Energy diversification & manufacturing minerals

Aerospace & Defense

Products: Aircraft parts, avionics, defense equipment

Trade Value: Fast-growing export segment

Opportunity: Make in India, offsets, MRO expansion

Electronics & Telecom

Products: Telecom hardware, components, semiconductors

Opportunity: India manufacturing push, 5G rollout

Focus: Semiconductor mission & supply chains

Agri-Food & Beverages

Products: Tequila, mezcal, beer, processed foods

Trade Value: High-growth premium category

Opportunity: India’s premium spirits & retail expansion
