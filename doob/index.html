What is MIBC?

The México–India Business Council is the first dedicated bilateral business council for the México–India corridor based in India. Established with the support of the Government of México, the Council serves as the institutional platform for commercial engagement between the two nations. MIBC operates at the intersection of government policy and private enterprise-providing intelligence, introductions, and institutional support to navigate cross-border transactions. Our mandate extends beyond trade promotion to encompass investment facilitation, market entry support, and delegation coordination.

About MIBC

The México–India Corridor.

The Corridor USD 13 billion in bilateral trade. USD 4 billion in Indian investment in México. 200+ Indian companies operating in México across IT, pharmaceuticals, and automotive manufacturing. Yet the institutional infrastructure to support this corridor has been absent—until now. México offers USMCA access to 500 million consumers. India offers scale, technology, and a domestic market growing at 6–7% annually. MIBC exists to convert this complementarity into commercial outcomes.
